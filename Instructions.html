Su-Dope-Ku

How to play:

    Singleplayer Mode:

Objective: Fill each row, column and 3x3 box with one of each digit from 1 to 9. There may not be any duplicate numbers in each row, column or box.

Controls: move the mouse around and click on an available cell to type in a number.

Timer: From the moment the game starts the timer begins to count up. Finish the puzzle as fast as you can to get the most points.

    Multiplayer Mode:

Objective: Fill each row, column and 3x3 box with one of each digit from 1 to 9. There may not be any duplicate numbers in each row, column or box.

Controls: move the mouse around and click on an available cell to type in a number.

Timer: From the moment the game starts the timer begins to count up. Finish the puzzle faster than your opponent to win.
